About

Research at the intersection of economics and geopolitics

I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at Université Paris 8. My research interests include international trade, geoeconomics, political economy, geopolitical risk, conflict economics, and maritime trade.

My doctoral research, Trade Shocks and Geoeconomic Fragmentation, studies how geopolitical shocks and institutional fragmentation affect shipping networks, market outcomes, and sourcing strategies. My work combines applied econometrics with large-scale trade, maritime, price, and conflict datasets.
